Tenchi Muyo: Manifest Destiny

By Courtney M. "Black Raptress" Downs (QueenIsis94@aol.com)
  • Chapter 1 : "Enter Karasu"
    An Earthling woman becomes the newest resident at the Masaki household. Tenchi is glad to have someone normal staying with him, but Karasu turns out to be anything but! 17KB -10/8/2001
  • Chapter 2 : "Kamikaze Kiyone"
    Kiyone is called to Earth with orders to bring Mihoshi home from Earth, where she has been "kidnapped," but Kiyone has other ideas for her old partner. 25KB -12/29/2001

  • Chapter 3 : "The Prophecy"
    Tenchi and the gang visit a natural history museum, and Kiyone, Tenchi, and Karasu get into a fight with a special section of the Galaxy Police who want to capture Ryoko and take Mihoshi home! 29KB -12/29/2001

  • Chapter 4 : "The Trouble With Dinosaurs"
    A strange animal is raiding farms around the Masaki Shrine. As Shinto Priests, Tenchi and Katsuhito go to investigate. 49KB -12/29/2001

  • Chapter 5 : "Ayeka and Tokimi"
    Due to an information-gathering foray by Washu and Karasu, Ayeka ends up succumbing to an illness she has been fighting off for weeks. 47KB -12/29/2001

  • Chapter 6 : "Sting Operation!"
    The true kidnappers, three Spycatchers working for the Marshal, land on Earth and successfully kidnap Mihoshi and Ryoko, with Karasu as a bonus prize. How will the Masaki family rescue its missing members? 50KB -12/29/2001

  • Chapter 7 : "Trojan Yugami!"
    Tenchi and the remaining members of the Masaki family set out to rescue Ryoko, Karasu and Mihoshi, but do they really need rescuing? Special bonus cameo by Amane Kaunaku of the Tenchi Muyo! GXP series! 49KB -1/30/2002

  • Chapter 8 : "Galaxy Police Station Seven"
    Reunited, the gang fights against the quirks of the Galaxy Police to win their freedom and restore their good names! Tenchi makes the Light Hawk Wings! 49KB -5/8/2002

  • Chapter 9 : "A Slice of Life"
    It's a normal day at the Masaki house--unfortunately. Between Karasu's new karaoke machine, Ryoko's threats to blow up Tenchi's school, Sasami's suprise dinner, Washu's weird experiments with Ayeka, Tenchi's grueling new training, and Karasu's plot to remove Mihoshi's ribbon, the boy who can make the Light Hawk Wings will have his hands full! 55KB -9/13/2002
