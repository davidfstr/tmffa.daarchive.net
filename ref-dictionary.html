Dictionary

A collection of some terms that are frequently used. More terms will be added progressively.

avatar
The author uses the character's name as his or her email address, then the character is an avatar, a rule of thumb though not an exact definition since it can vary.

crossover
A fic that involves more then one universe/story. eg. X-files and Ranma 1/2. These stories tend to change the dynamics of the worlds concerned since it involves characters that were not probably going to meet.

dark
A fan fic that does not have a happy ending.  Or the mood/feel of the story is not happy.

fusion
A type of crossover fan fiction, where you take character from one series, but cast them into the parts of another.

lemon
A fan fic that has adult/sexual content. This term is derived from a anime called "Cream Lemon." Synonyms: Hentai, Ecchi

lime
Not as strong as a lemon, but still does contain adult/sexual content that may not be appropriate for minors.

MST
A critical review of a fanfic.  This term is derived from the show "Mystery Science Theater 3000" Synonyms: MST3K, MSTs, MSTing

omake
Side stories that are not canon to main story archs.

prereader
A person that can checks an authors story for flaws before he/she publishes it.  They check for grammatical errors, see if a character is in character, give the author feedback on their story. A very useful resource.

revenge fics
Usually a spoof done by the author of a particular story where his characters come to the real world and try to take revenge on him.

self insertion
When the author places himself/herself in the story.  Synonyms: SI

spam fic
A short story usually used to demonstrate a theory or idea.

WAFF
Acronym for Warm And Fuzzy Feeling. A story that has a good ending and is filled with happiness and joy making you warm inside. It usually has either romance or cuteness.

yaoi
A type of fan fic that displays a male and male relationship.

yuri
A type of fan fic that displays a female and female relationship.
