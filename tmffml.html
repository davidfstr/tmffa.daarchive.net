Purpose

This list is mainly an announcement list. You will receive a E-mail when I have updated my page and when there are important announcements. The traffic from this mailing list should be pretty small, since it is mainly an announcement list. Average mail load is two to ten E-mail messages a month.

When I do inform you of the update, I will try to release a short newsletter which includes some trivia, previews, questionares, information, and other stuff.

Subscribe

Subscription is done by sending mail to tmffml-request@mikomi.org with "subscribe" in the subject or the body of the message, or by sending mail to listar@mikomi.org with "subscribe tmffml" in the subject or the body.

Unsubscribe

If you wish to remove yourself from the list, unsubscription is done by sending mail to tmffml-request@mikomi.org with "unsubscribe" in the subject or the body, or by sending mail to listar@mikomi.org with "unsubscribe tmffml" in the subject or the body.

Problems

If you have any problems or would like me to manually add or remove you from the mailing list, E-mail me at GenSao@tmffa.com.
