Voting for TMFFA T-Shirt Design

The winner of this contest will win a TMFFA T-Shirt that they have designed.  Pleas vote for the best design IYHO

Introduction

The design of the TMFFA will now be selected by you, the readers/writers of the TMFFA.  This is your T-shirt, so chose wisely.

The Artist will receive a free TMFFA T-shirt as their reward for wining the contest. The winning design will then be used on the back of the T-Shirt.  Also the winning image will be used on the Main start up page for the TMFFA.

Rules for Voting

Please E-mail your votes to gensao@tmffa.com.  Just select the design number that you like.  Simplistically write "I want T-shirt Design XX" with XX being the shirt design number in the header and body of the letter.  One vote per person, or all your votes will be disqualified!  You may not vote for your own design.

Votes are accepted now!  The last day to send in your vote is November 21, 2000!

If you have preordered before, then your vote will count 5X.  Not 2X, by 5X.  I decided to up the power of your vote since you ultimately will be wearing the T-Shirt.  Please make a note of this when you do E-mail your vote.

You can still preorder to get your vote to count 5X!
If you have yet to preorder your T-shirt, there is still some time to get your vote to count 5X. You can use PayPal (Account: gensao@yahoo.com) to quickly get your order in. Just make a note in your E-mail when you do this. You may still also send in a check or money order, but it must be received before I tally the votes a few days after the last day of votes to be accepted.  This is a limited time offer.  Upon confirmation of payment, your vote will count 5X.
