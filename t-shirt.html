The TMFFA T-Shirt!

The TMFFA will have its own T-shirt!

Introduction

This has been on my mind some time. I have bounced the idea around a few people, and they all have been generally positive. Now I will make it a reality. I will be producing a TMFFA T-shirt. It will be a white 100% cotton heavyweight T-shirt with a small tmffa.com logo on the front and a fan art/design on the back with the phrase "The Tenchi Muyo Fan Fiction Archive.  The logos will be silk screened.

All funds collected in this limited print run will pay for future prizes and giveaways. Like my lofty idea of giving away the Tenchi Muyo OAV DVD Collection to the 1,000,000th Visitor to the TMFFA!

Unfortunately, I do not have the shirts at the moment, since I need a back design. So I decided to have a contest!  After the winner has been decided, The production process will begin.  More information will be added to this page as information becomes available.

If you do preorder NOW!  Your vote will count 5X in the contest!

NOTE:   I currently have only 1 short-sleeve-S and 1 XXL T-shirts available in stock at the moment.
