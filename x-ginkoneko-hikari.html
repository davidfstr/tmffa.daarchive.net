Ginkoneko Hikari

By Saiyan Moon Goddess (ryoko@coqui.net)
  • Prologue
    What if a new girl with silver hair and golden eyes goes to the same school as Trunks, Bra, and Goten, and falls in love with Trunks? Romance/Mystery/Drama/Angst. 6KB -9/13/2002
  • Chapter 1 : "They Meet"
    The new girl arrives at Trunks' school. Trunks invites her for dinner at Capsule Corp, which turns out to be a disaster food race between saiyans and an unusual silver haired, golden eyed teenager. 17KB 9/13/2002

    Trunks goes to Ginkoneko's house. What? Ginkoneko likes to draw? Who is the mysterious woman that appears in all her drawings? 20KB -9/13/2002

    Trunks and Ginkoneko's first date. At the beach! 18KB -9/13/2002

    The date continues, weird things happen. 16KB -9/13/2002
  • Chapter 5 : "Ginkoneko's Dream/Nightmare"
    Ginkoneko's training starts. Ginkoneko starts to have weird nightmares and visions about something. Why? 24KB -9/13/2002
  • Chapter 6 : "Reflections and Opinions"
    Ginkoneko talks to her friends about her dreams and nightmares. Each of them give their opinion to try and solve the mystery. 3KB -9/13/2002
  • Chapter 7 : "A Letter of Confesions and Revelations"
    Ginkoneko's birthday is near, Trunks will ask the BIG question, when a tragedy rips things apart!! Guess who comes back to visit! 24KB -9/13/2002
  • Chapter 8 : "The Lament of the Ghost"
    Ginkoneko runs into the forest. Silver Light finds her and tells her about a roaming ghost in the forest, but Silver Light is acting strange. 17KB -9/13/2002
  • Chapter 9 : "Ginkoneko's "Friend". Who is Silver Light?"
    Ginkoneko and Trunks get married. But someone is collecting the dragonballs! Silver Light knows who Ginkoneko's mother is. Will she reveal her as well as a secret of her own? 21KB -9/13/2002
  • Chapter 10 : "Scars >From The Past"
    Ginkoneko's mother explains part of her horrible past. When she doesn't finish, Tsunami helps her. 28KB -9/13/2002
